What is CET Day?

CET Day is the annual college celebration of the College of Engineering and Technology. It is a day where students, faculty, and staff come together to celebrate unity, excellence, talent, leadership, and innovation.

Every CET Day is the annual college celebration of the College of Engineering and Technology. It is a day where students, faculty, and staff come together to celebrate unity, excellence, talent, leadership, and innovation.

The celebration highlights the strengths of the two major programs: BS in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) and BS in Information Technology (BSIT). Each program proudly showcases its skills, creativity, and competitive spirit.

Contested Activities & Major Events

  • Mr. and Ms. CET Pageant
  • Visual Arts
  • Programming
  • Mobile Legends Tournament
  • Promotional Video Competition
  • Mr. & Ms. CET Pageant
  • Cultural Games

CET Day is more than just a celebration — it is a symbol of teamwork, determination, and the passion of future engineers and IT professionals. It strengthens camaraderie, builds confidence, and inspires students to pursue excellence in both academics and life.
